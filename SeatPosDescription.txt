Car designers want to know where different drivers will position their seat. Researchers at the University of Michigan collected data on 38 drivers, and use the variable "hipcenter" as a measure of the position of the seat (i.e. this is the dependent variable Y).

Age: Age in years
Weight: Weight in lbs
HtShoes: Height with shoes on in cm
Ht: Height when bare foot in cm
Seated: Seated height in cm
Arm: lower arm length in cm
Thigh: Thigh length in cm
Leg: Lower leg length in cm
hipcenter: horizontal distance of the midpoint of the hips from a
fixed location in the car in mm.
